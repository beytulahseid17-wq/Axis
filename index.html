Privacy Policy

Axis stores the data you enter — habits, check-ins, financial figures, trips, and goals — in your own account, in a database only you can access. Row-level security rules mean no other user can ever read or write your data.

Your email and password are handled by Supabase Authentication and are never stored in plain text. Financial figures you enter are for your own tracking only — Axis does not connect to real bank accounts or move money.

If you use the AI coach, the messages you send and your recent habit data are sent to the AI provider (currently Google Gemini) to generate a response. This is used only to produce your reply.

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from Settings.

Terms & Conditions

Axis is provided as-is, without warranty of any kind. Financial figures, goals, and trip plans you enter are for personal organization only and do not constitute financial or travel advice.

You're responsible for the accuracy of the data you enter. Axis does not verify balances, transactions, or goal figures against any external source.

The AI coach's suggestions are generated by a third-party AI model and may be inaccurate — use your own judgment, especially for anything financial or health-related.

FAQ

Is my data private?
Yes — each account's data is isolated at the database level, enforced by row-level security.

Does Axis connect to my real bank account?
No. The Financial tab is a manual tracker — you enter your own numbers.

What happens if I go offline?
Axis is installable as an app and caches itself for offline use.

Can I remove ads?
Yes — ads only show on the free plan. Upgrading to Premium removes them.
